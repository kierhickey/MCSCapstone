Rental Costs

Melbourne Consulting Suites offers competitive rental rates for top quality rooms. We offer fixed rates for all our rooms. Our casual base rate is $15 per hour, however our rate drops to $10 per hour for weekly recurring bookings or block bookings (half/full-days, evenings). You can book any combination of the above, and have casual bookings as required which you can access yourself on-line. The on-line system is very easy to use. All pricing excludes GST.
