Rental Costs

Rental rates for our Consulting Suites are highly competitive, particularly in consideration of the rooms’ quality.

  • Casual hourly rate
    $15 (+gst)
  • Evening (5.30pm – 8.30pm)
    $30 (+gst)
  • Half day (7.30am – 12.30pm  or  12.30pm – 5.30pm)
    $50 (+gst)
  • Full Day (7.30am – 5.30pm)
    $100 (+gst)

You can book any combination of the above, and have casual bookings as required which you can access yourself on-line. The on-line system is very easy to use.
