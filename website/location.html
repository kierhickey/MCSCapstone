Parking

Abundant parking is available for both consultants and their clients/patients directly outside. The options include 1 & 2 hour parking on St. Kilda Road, 1 hour parking on Commercial Road, 4 hour parking in adjacent Hanna Street.

There are also a number of commercial car parks (next door & across the road) which provide competitive daily rates ($16, $22) and scaled monthly rates.

Location

Melbourne Consulting Suites is central, accessible from inner Melbourne institutions and suburbs, and is convenient to outer Melbourne road and public transport connections. It is situated on a busy corner bustling with trams, busses, cafes on leafy St. Kilda Road. Our rooms are surrounded by Melbournes’ iconic city gardens (AlexandraGardens, The Botanical Gardens, FawknerPark, Albert Cricket Ground, AlbertParkLake. It is a short walk to exclusive Toorak Road, trendy Commercial Road and chic Prahran facilities (shops, businesses, restaurants).

Our rooms are located in one of Melbourne’s iconic medical buildings on Melbourne’s renowned and historical St. Kilda Road. A stone’s throw from the city, our rooms are situated within the busy business district with small to national and international businesses for neighbours (Alfred Hospital, Linfox, L’oreal, Yves Saint Laurent, Jacobs, AAMI, AIA Australia, Cadbury, Australasian College of Aneasthetists, Federal Police headquarters, Orica, VACC, St. Kilda Rd. Victoria Police Headquarters, Wesley College, Melbourne Grammar School).
