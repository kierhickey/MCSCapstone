Overview

Each room is a good size with high quality and attractive fit outs. Rooms have been designed with private and discrete consulting in mind. Highest level of visual and acoustic privacy is provided using highest grade sound proofing in walls and ceilings. Rooms are decorated with purpose specific comfortable and elegant leather furniture. Décor consists of neutral, quiet colour tones and furnishings which include desk with leather couch, arm chairs, window blinds, and appropriate wall hangings. Every room has a tap/basin for access to drinking water. Lamps provide mood lighting as an option and overhead room lights. The rooms emit a tranquillity and quiet as you enter.

Practitioner Facilities

  • Serviced professional consulting rooms.
  • HICAPS and EFTPOS facility.
  • Kitchenette for tea/coffee equipped with microwave, fridge, shredder.
  • Central air-conditioning and heating can be adjusted.
  • Private therapist only toilet.

 

Client Facilities

  • Separate male and female toilets
  • An abundance of coffee shops; chic and healthy lunch and dinner outlets and restaurants
  • Bike ramp
  • Automatic teller machine directly across the road
  • Huge comfortable waiting room with contemporary magazines
  • Handicap accessible facilities (ramps and lift)
